Uber (2025)

A danceable neotraditional tune, evoking lost love with "I would give anything to see you walk through that door." Lance's striking, one-of-a-kind vocal uses no auto-tune. Uber features Brent Mason (14-time ACM Guitarist, Grammy winner, recently working with Zack Top & Post Malone), Scotty Sanders (I Never Lie- Zach Top & steel guitar for Gene Watson, Dolly Parton), and Brian Brignac (drummer for Eric Clapton, Wayne Toups). Perfect for modern "Country Gold" & '90s playlists.

Too Drunk (2024)

Lance Woolie's debut release "Too Drunk" was created in collaboration the renowned Legendary Guitarist of the Year Brent Mason. It's worth noting that Lance came up with the initial tune while Jefferey Allen Imler was visiting a session in the studio. This unique combination of talent and creativity resulted in a remarkable piece of music that is sure to captivate audiences.

Worst Enemy (2023)

With raw and honest lyrics like “I’ll gouge out both my eyes then complain that I can’t see,” this song captures the struggles of self-sabotage. Set against the backdrop of Louisiana, with references to Bourbon Street and Mardi Gras, this song brings the vibrant southern culture to life. The soulful vocals and authentic instrumentation add to the rich tapestry of the story, taking listeners on a journey through the highs and lows of the human experience. 50 time Grammy Award winner. Brent Mason's lead guitar adds a perfect blend for fans of classic country music and those looking for a song that speaks modern truths of the heart.

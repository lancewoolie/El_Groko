Project Goals

Build a sleek, interactive website (lancewoolie.com / El_Groko GitHub repo) as a digital illustration of "The Moon Saloon" – the first off-Earth permanent saloon on Alpha Base Camp (Moon's South Pole).
Integrate NASA's Artemis Plan for continuity: South Pole (Shackleton Crater) location with water/ice volatiles for ISRU (oxygen, fuel, water), LTV rovers for mobility, habitable platforms for long stays, robotic precursors (VIPER, CLPS landers), power systems (solar/fission), and international/commercial partners (Canada robotics, Japan habitation, ESA refueling, SpaceX logistics).
Make it engaging: Shooting gallery game with 1/6 gravity, shootable props (nav links), high-score leaderboard (Firebase free tier for fan marks).
Fan hub: Congregate area (guestbook/graffiti via Firebase), upcoming shows (events page), music/videos (YouTube embeds), merch sales (Bandcamp off-site), contact/bookings (EmailJS free tier), updates signup (Firebase free tier).
Rules: Eternally free hosting (GitHub Pages), free 3rd parties only (Firebase, EmailJS), secure/responsive/sexy/fast (mobile-first Bootstrap, optimized PNGs), merch/videos off-site.
